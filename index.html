Redwake Harbor
Aliases The Red Port + Add
Overview
Redwake Harbor is a coastal port city infamous for its tide — a slow, inevitable surge of seawater stained the colour of oxidized iron. The phenomenon, known locally as the Redwake, arrives with clockwork regularity and recedes just as calmly, leaving ships, docks, and conscience equally marked.

To merchants, the Redwake is a necessary risk. To theologians, a contradiction that refuses explanation.
Geography & the Redwake
The harbour sits in a shallow bay shaped like a broken crescent, sheltered from violent storms yet perpetually overcast. The Redwake tide rolls in once every nine days, beginning as a faint discolouration at the horizon before deepening into a rust-red swell. The Larkspire lighthouse marks the northern approach.

The Shadow War
The conflict that would come to be known as the Shadow War began not with armies, but with a single act of betrayal at the Council of the Obsidian Tower.

When Mira the Wise refused the pact, the Winterborn Clan saw no choice but to march south. Their grievance was ancient — older than the Tower itself — and it would not be settled with words.

The first battle was fought at Frostfire Village, where the defenders held for three days before the walls finally fell. It was here that the legend of the Shieldmaiden began.
